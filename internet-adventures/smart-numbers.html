Adventure 3 - Smart Numbers

Let's take the CAS number for my favorite substance benzene: 71-43-2

71-43-2
-------
3*1 + 4*2 + 1*3 + 7*4 =
3   + 8   + 3   + 28  = 42
                      = 2 (mod 10)
