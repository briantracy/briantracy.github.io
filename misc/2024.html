2024

2023 was a another transitional year for me. I moved back to California half way through the year and changed jobs in the process. I took a more disciplined approach to doing the things I find healthy and felt proud of my effort. I want to continue my positive momentum in 2024, but with more focus on fewer goals.

New 2024 Goals

  • Athletic
    • Squat+Deadlift+Bench Press 1000lbs
    • Run 2 miles in 12 minutes
    • Olympic length triathlon
    • Plank 7 minutes
  • Personal
    • Form closer friendships with coworkers
    • Continue to reduce procrastination
    • Become a better active listener
    • Be a student in some capacity
  • Creative
    • Perform music for an audience
    • Complete 2 medium scale projects (software or other)
    • Write and share 2 short stories
